Two-Tone Skillet Kebab

Two-tone skillet kebab is one of the delicious and festive Iranian dishes, prepared with a variety of recipes. This dish is a combination of red meat and chicken skillet kebabs, and because of its festive appearance, it can be a great choice for gatherings and parties. You can make this tasty kebab in various designs, and if you want a simpler version for a casual dinner or lunch, you can prepare it in a simpler way. However, for a more elegant presentation, you can shape it in molds. Follow along with the Iranian cooking tutorial on our website.
