Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets – 9 pieces
  • Ground beef – 300 grams
  • Onion – 1 piece
  • Tomato sauce – 1 cup
  • Pizza cheese – as needed
  • Spices, salt, and pepper – to taste

Instructions:

Chop and sauté the onion, then add the ground beef. Once the beef is cooked, add the tomato sauce and let it simmer to develop the flavors. Prepare the lasagna sheets according to the instructions on the package. In an appropriate dish, layer the lasagna, meat, and cheese. Bake at 180°C for about 30 minutes until the cheese is golden and fragrant.
