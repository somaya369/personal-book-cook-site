Veggie Burger

Delicious homemade veggie burger made with black beans and spices.

Veggie Burger

Ingredients:

  • Black beans
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Spices (cumin, paprika)
  • Olive oil

Directions:

Mash beans and mix with breadcrumbs, veggies, and spices. Form into patties and cook in olive oil.
